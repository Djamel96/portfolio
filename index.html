Makelti

Makelti is an information system to manage catering in companies, eliminate the waste of meals, improve service for employees and improve the turnover of the company The system consists of a web dashboard for accounts, menus and kitchen management, and a mobile application for the employees available for Android and iOS mobiles.


Mavor

Mavor is an Android and iOS mobile application that allows individuals and companies to organise events, the participants will find all the organised events and choose by swiping left if not interested or right if want to attend. The participant can chat in realtime with the organiser, so if he is interested, he proceed to payement using his payment card in the app


Vakoli

Vakoli is mobile application that allows you to find company in your travels. It is so simple to use and very practical. You have just to shedule a travel and find other travelers with the same tavel details. So you swipe left or right to choose people you want to meet and chat with them. You can also share your travels memories with your firends within awesome posts !
