Mavor

Mavor is an Android and iOS mobile application that allows individuals and companies to organise events, the participants will find all the organised events and choose by swiping left if not interested or right if want to attend. The participant can chat in realtime with the organiser, so if he is interested, he proceed to payement using his payment card in the app
